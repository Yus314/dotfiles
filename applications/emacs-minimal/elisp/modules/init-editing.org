* Meow
Keep the minimal profile's modal layer deliberately small.  These bindings
preserve the DVP-oriented movement layout from the full profile, but omit
unrelated structural-editing and package-specific commands.  Lowercase ~g~ is
left unbound for the guarded selection-batch prefix; uppercase ~G~ remains
~meow-grab~.
